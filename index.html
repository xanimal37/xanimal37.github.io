Art Shop

Welcome to the Inorganometallic Catalyst Design Center (ICDC), Nanoporous Materials Genome Center (NMGC) and UMN Materials Research Science & Engineering Center (MRSEC) 'Art Studio'.

This site was created to give members of the centers mentioned above an idea of the kinds of products that can be requested for fully or partially funded research projects. In addition to images and figures, I have also created videos and animations. I am looking for chances to push my skills further both in conceptual ideas for covers and in the areas of animation and 3d on the web.

If you have a project that needs graphics, please fill out the form here. If you are looking for a random image to use as a background or maybe an intro page to a presention, see the art store library.

If you would like to discuss a potential project, please contact me at zeeb0002@umn.edu

All images, videos and other artwork on this site is © University of Minnesota.
