welcome

This site was created to give members of the centers mentioned above an idea of the kinds of products that can be requested for fully or partially funded research projects. In addition to images and figures, I have also created videos and animations. I am looking for chances to push my skills further both in conceptual ideas for covers and in the areas of animation and 3d on the web.

If you have a project that needs graphics, please fill out the form here. If you are looking for a random image to use as a background or maybe an intro page to a presention, see the library.

If you would like to discuss a potential project, please contact me at zeeb0002@umn.edu

The graphics contained on this web site are for the sole use of members of the Inorganometallic Catalyst Design Center (ICDC), Nanoporous Materials Genome Center (NMGC) and the University of Minnesota Materials Research Science & Engineering Center (UMN MRSEC). All work must be entirely or partially funded by the respective center.
